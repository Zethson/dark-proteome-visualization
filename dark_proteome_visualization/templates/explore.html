Parallel Coordinates

Parallel Coordinates provide a great way of visualizing correlations in high-dimensional spaces. Because of the size of the dataset that is visualized, individual lines are barely distinguishable. Therefore, the opacity is set to 0.01. This enables to get a feeling for the distributions and the correlations of subsets of certain dimensions.
